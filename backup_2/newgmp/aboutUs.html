About Us

Founded in 2014, GETMYPARCEL.COM is a web based e-commerce parcel comparison website offering high quality discounted courier service for individuals, small /large business firms. Our website enables you to send anything, from a document to a large parcel, throughout the world and UK, all for lowest industry rates. Once you log into our website all our services are bookable online and you can instantly compare quotes for a variety of services.

Why Use GETMYPARCEL.COM ?

We're are fast growing to become the UKs leading e-commerce website which helps individual, businesses, Amazon, and other marketplace sellers save thousands of pounds every day. Anybody can sign up with us and start benefiting from our great discounts right away . We have one of the cheapest prices for UK & International deliveries.

  1. Provides simple & affordable logistics services to customers and businesses.
  2. Is a price comparison website, where customers can choose from a variety of of top carriers and suppliers.
  3. You can book online and we will arrange pickup from your Home (or) Work. It's that simple and we assure you ones you use our service you will come back to us.


Our website enables customers to send standard & non-standard products, in variety of sizes, to about 220+ countries (almost any) destinations worldwide. We have the best customer services team available every day to guide you from booking your parcel, through payments and to track your parcel and resolve any issues you may have. This means that by using GETMYPARCEL.COM you can take advantage for everything from booking, collection through to tracking and all with the cheapest rates and with the best service. This means unlike other parcel comparison sites we provide everything under one roof.
