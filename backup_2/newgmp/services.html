Service Details

GetMyParcel.com offers a low cost, fast and easy same day courier service for customized delivery of emergency shipments within UK mainland (excluding remote areas).

It is a next available truck delivery service to any region within UK mainland in the shortest possible time. Our suppliers guarantee door-to-door delivery and same day pickup service. It takes less than 40 seconds* to get a same day courier quote using your very User friendly www.GetMyParcel.com account and the collection can be made as early as within 60 minutes (1 hour).

Your package will be collected and taken straight to the delivery address. The weight limit for individual items is 30kg. There is also a volumetric limit which will be calculated when you give the dimensions of your parcel during the booking process.

Our Products Portfolio

Currently there are many other products which support delivery from next day - to within a few days. We do not have any products in our portfolio which support same day services for the moment. We are working towards getting these services made available to you in near future. Please refer to any of our other service which provide a detailed list of products to support your courier needs.

Go to GetMyParcel Home page to generate a quote now.

Notes:

  • You will be required to attach to your parcel, a print out of the shipping label.
  • Do ensure that your parcel does not contain any items mentioned under "Prohibited items list".
