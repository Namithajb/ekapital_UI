IT & Software Recruitment Services

  • Home

  • Software Recruitment Service

We leverage our Niche IT capability to identify resources with the right skill set for our clients with experience in the domain of choice. What started as a add-on services where Clients requested for team members with specific skill sets to augument their existing resource pool, ended up as a Service offering. Our Experienced team of recruiters and the channel connections now provide a well oiled Onboarding service – of working with for our clients, to identify, interview and onboard team members.

Responsible Onboarding

Our customers can focus on their Business priorities, while we search and engage with candidates with right technological skills, optionally interview them and enable all processes until onboarding on behalf of our customer.

Relationship Onboarding

We not only focus on identifying candidates and onboarding them to our Client teams but also perform all due deligence necessary to ensure the right candidate is onboarded including performing background verifications and remuneration negotiations as applicable.

Our Value Additions

  • Roles at All Levels

    We work with All levels - from Senior Managers, Senior Consultants to all ranges of Architects to Software Engineers, Testers, Business Analysts and SMEs. Knowledge in handling and dealing with all range of Software based positions makes us Unique in our proposition.

  • Knowledgeable Team for Technology Suitability

    We have a highly technology aware Recruitment and Onboarding team with hands on Technical Architects, who advice our Recruitment Consultants of the appropriateness of a candidate for a Job role, ensuring perfect match to client requirements.

  • Interview Options

    We provide option for our Customers to undertake an initial Technical interview to ensure only right candidtes are put forward for the right job role, ensuring better turnaround and best use of our customers time

  • Background Checks and Due Diligence

    Our services include Background checks and other Due diligence for the candidates to ensure only the right candidates get to attend inteviews and get recruited
