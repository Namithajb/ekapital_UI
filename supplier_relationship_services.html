Supplier Relationship Services

  • Home
  • Services
  • Supplier Relationship Service

As part of the Supplier relationship Management logistics service, our team will manage all relationships with the suppliers including rates negotiation, account management, day to day operations, payments matching, and reporting. All aspects of transportation flow, including delivery from suppliers to warehouses and from warehouses to customers are managed in a changing business environment in association with suppliers.

Right Business Priorities

Our customers can focus on their right priorities to improve sales, while we engage with 3PL suppliers and manage logistics on behalf of the business, including supplier relationship management and reporting.

Relationship Priorities

Our customers focus on relationships which improve their business sales and reach, while we focus on relationships with suppliers, thus ensuring the back office functions are executed like a well-oiled machine.

Included Services

  • Supplier Relationship Management

    Relationships with all transportation and warehouse suppliers, including rates negotiation, supplier reporting, realtime progress tracking, IT build and configuration among others.

    • Planning and Reporting

    Daily, weekly and Quarterly reporting, Operations planning and management, Business growth planning, scheduling, project delivery and rollout training.

Customised Solutions

All solutions are customizable to suit to customer's needs. On a case by case basis and the nature of business, each of the solutions are to be adapted for best output. Some of the benefits of this service are:

  • Single point of management and reporting
  • Reliable and Predictable Business model
  • Flexible based on changing industry changes
  • Focus on growth priorities while we handle operations
